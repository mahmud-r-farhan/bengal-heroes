Introduction

Welcome to Bengal Heroes, a free, offline-first educational mobile application crafted by Bengal Bytes to honor and preserve the legacy of Bengal's greatest historical figures — freedom fighters, intellectuals, poets, rulers, and leaders.

This application is offered solely for educational purposes and operates without advertisements, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. By using Bengal Heroes, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy and agree to comply with the Terms of Service outlined herein.

Core Principles
• No Personal Data Required — No email, name, phone, or account creation needed
• No Permissions Required — App requests no special device permissions
• Offline-First Design — All core features work completely offline
• No Advertising — Zero ads, tracking, or commercial data collection
• Free Forever — No in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid features
• No Data Selling — Your usage data is never sold or shared

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect & Use

No Personal Information Required
Bengal Heroes requires ZERO personal information to download, install, or use. No email address, name, phone number, location, or account credentials are ever requested or collected.

The app collects only minimal technical data required for core functionality. The vast majority of your data never leaves your device.

What Data Is Stored Locally on Your Device

  • App Preferences — Language selection (English/Bengali), theme preference, font size
  • Saved Favorites — Your bookmarked heroes (stored in device's encrypted storage)
  • Search History — Local search queries (never transmitted)
  • Cached Content — Downloaded images and biographical data for offline access

What Optional Data May Be Collected

Bengal Heroes may collect anonymized, non-personal technical information to improve performance and stability:

  • Anonymous Crash Reports — App errors (no personal identifiers attached)
  • Performance Metrics — Feature usage frequencies, app response times
  • Device Compatibility — Device model, OS version, screen size (for optimization)

None of this data is linked to your identity, stored on remote servers for extended periods, or used for marketing. We use this information exclusively to maintain stability, fix bugs, and improve performance.

App Permissions: What We Request (and What We Don't)

  • ✓ INTERNET (Optional) — Used only for optional features in future updates
  • ✗ NO location access
  • ✗ NO contacts or calendar access
  • ✗ NO camera or microphone
  • ✗ NO SMS or call logs
  • ✗ NO account credentials or sign-in

Data Retention & Your Rights

Most data never leaves your device. Favorites, preferences, and cached content are stored locally in encrypted storage managed by your operating system.

On Your Device

Uninstalling the app automatically removes all local data including favorites, preferences, search history, and cached content. You can also clear app data manually through your device settings.

Data We Store on Our Servers

Bengal Bytes stores minimal data on secure servers, limited to:

  • Support Communications — If you email us, we retain your message for 60 days after resolution, then auto-delete
  • Anonymized Analytics — Aggregate feature usage (not linked to individuals), retained for 90 days
  • Error Logs — Crash reports (anonymized), retained for 30 days for debugging

Your Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA Compliance)

  • Right to Access: Request what data we have on you
  • Right to Deletion: Request removal of any data you've provided
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in standard format
  • Right to Opt-Out: Disable crash reporting any time in app settings

Submit requests via email to support@bengalbytes.com with subject "Data Request". We respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Bengal Heroes is specifically designed to be safe for all ages, including children under 13. The app is an ideal learning tool for students in schools and home education.

Child Safety Commitments:
• No personal information collection from anyone, including minors
• No account, login, or email required
• No external web browsing or links to inappropriate content
• No collection of children's data for behavioral tracking
• Historical content reviewed for educational appropriateness
• Compliant with Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of personal data from children, we will delete it immediately (within 24 hours).

For Parents/Guardians: Bengal Heroes contains no ads, no in-app purchases, and no external tracking. It is completely safe for children to use independently or under supervision.
